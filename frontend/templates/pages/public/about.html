About MavunoRoute AI

We are building Kenya's first agri-logistics operating system to coordinate perishable produce movement from farms to markets.

Our Mission

Reduce post-harvest losses by connecting farmers directly to buyers, optimizing transport routes, and ensuring cold chain integrity from farm to fork.

MavunoRoute AI brings together farmers, buyers, transporters, and cold hub operators on a single platform with real-time coordination, deterministic spoilage prediction, and seamless payment integration including M-Pesa.
